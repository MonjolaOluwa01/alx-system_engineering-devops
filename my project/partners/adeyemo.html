Adeyemo A. Adebayo

Adeyemo Adebayo is a Fellow of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria and a Fellow of the Chartered Institute of Taxation of Nigeria, a Certified Forensic Accountant, as well as an Associate Member of the Association of Certified Fraud Examiners (USA).

Adeyemo is a 1985 Psychology graduate of the University of Lagos. He commenced his professional training in 1987 with the firm of Coopers and Lybrand (Chartered Accountants) now PriceWater HouseCoopers, where he qualified as a Chartered Accountant in 1991.

Yemo established Adeyemo Adebayo & Co in 1998 and brought to Abax-OOSA Professionals 16 years post-qualification experience in Auditing, Forensic Accounting, Taxation and Advisory Services.
